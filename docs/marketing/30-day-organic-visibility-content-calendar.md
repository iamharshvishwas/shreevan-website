# Shreevan Wellness 30-Day Organic Visibility Content Calendar

Status: No-ads acceleration plan
Prepared: 2026-06-27
Primary goal: Get Shreevan Wellness noticed faster by Google Search, AI search surfaces, social search and referral audiences without paid ads.
Target countries: United States, United Kingdom, Canada
Primary conversion: free suitability consultation

## Core Strategy

This is not a launch calendar and not a random social calendar. This is a 30-day organic visibility system for a new website.

The objective is to create early discoverability signals across:

- Google Search.
- Google AI Overviews and answer-style results.
- ChatGPT/Perplexity-style answer engines through clear, citable content.
- Instagram and LinkedIn social search.
- Referral discovery through partnerships, communities and directories.
- CRM lead attribution.

Because the website is new, fast organic traffic will not come from articles alone. The first 30 days should combine:

1. Indexing and crawl signals.
2. High-intent journal articles.
3. Short AEO answer assets.
4. Internal links into program pages.
5. Country-specific trust content for USA, UK and Canada visitors.
6. Founder/team credibility content.
7. Daily social distribution with search-friendly captions.
8. Referral outreach to relevant directories, communities and collaborators.
9. CRM source tracking.

## 30-Day Success Definition

Do not expect rankings for competitive terms in 30 days. The realistic goal is early visibility and measurable signals.

By Day 30, aim for:

- 8 major journal articles published.
- 8 short answer assets or FAQ-style pages/sections published.
- 30 social distribution posts across Instagram and LinkedIn.
- 20-30 outreach attempts to relevant directories, blogs, studios or collaborators.
- Sitemap submitted and updated.
- Every new article internally linked from at least 3 existing pages.
- Search Console impressions starting for long-tail queries.
- CRM source tracking active for organic search, Instagram, LinkedIn and referral.
- First 1-5 organic or social-assisted consultation requests possible.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- No miracle claims.
- No cure claims.
- No fake urgency.
- No "best retreat" self-promotion without objective selection criteria.
- No generic wellness quotes as core content.
- Every article must include a direct answer in the first 70 words.
- Every article must link to one program page, one trust page and `/book-consultation`.
- Every social post must send users to a specific article, program page or consultation step.
- Every CTA should be consultation-first, not payment-first.

## Social Search Caption Formula

Use this format for Instagram and LinkedIn captions:

1. Start with the search phrase naturally.
2. State the problem clearly.
3. Give 3-5 practical points.
4. Add Shreevan-specific point of view.
5. End with one CTA.

Example:

`If you are comparing a 14-day vs 28-day retreat in India, the real question is not which is better. The real question is how much structure, emotional space and integration support you can realistically hold right now. At Shreevan Wellness, we use a suitability call before payment so the program depth matches the guest.`

## What Not To Do

- Do not publish low-quality daily blogs just to increase volume.
- Do not chase broad keywords like `yoga benefits`.
- Do not post random inspirational quotes.
- Do not create thin country pages.
- Do not push users to payment before suitability.
- Do not use AI-generated claims that sound medical.
- Do not ignore Search Console for 30 days.

## Recommendation

This 30-day calendar should be executed before creating a separate generic social calendar.

Reason:

- It creates real search assets.
- It gives social content a strategic base.
- It supports the program pages.
- It creates answer-led content for AI search.
- It gives CRM measurable source data.
- It does not depend on paid ads.

The first article should be:

`How to choose between a 3, 7, 14, 28 or 60-day wellness retreat`

It is the highest-leverage first asset because it supports every program, every CTA and every serious visitor who is still unsure.
