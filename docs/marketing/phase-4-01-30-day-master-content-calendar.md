# Phase 4-01: Shreevan Wellness 30-Day Master Content Calendar

Status: Planning deliverable  
Prepared: 2026-06-27  
Purpose: Create the first 30-day publishing calendar for Shreevan Wellness across journal, social, email, FAQ and conversion-support content.

## Scope

This is the Phase 4-01 master content calendar. It is not a paid ads plan and not a launch-announcement campaign. It is the first month of structured content publishing for a new premium wellness retreat website.

Primary goals:

1. Build topical authority for Shreevan Wellness.
2. Help USA, UK and Canada visitors understand the retreat offer.
3. Support `/programs/*`, `/modalities/*`, `/journal/*` and `/book-consultation` without keyword cannibalization.
4. Create AEO/GEO/AIO-friendly content that answers real user questions clearly.
5. Move visitors from education to program comparison to suitability consultation.

## Content Rules

- `/modalities/*` owns educational practice intent.
- `/programs/*` owns commercial and duration-based intent.
- `/journal/*` owns questions, comparisons, planning and objection handling.
- `/book-consultation` owns conversion intent.
- `/payment` stays for confirmed guests only and should not be promoted as a cold CTA.
- Every major journal article needs a direct answer in the first 70 words.
- Every high-intent article links to `/book-consultation`.
- Every claim must stay responsible: no cure claims, no miracle detox claims, no fake urgency.

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Draft | Topic approved, content not written |
| Review | Content written, needs brand/legal/SEO review |
