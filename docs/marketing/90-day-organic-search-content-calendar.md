# Shreevan Wellness 90-Day Organic Search Content Calendar

Status: Strategy draft  
Prepared: 2026-06-27  
Primary objective: Build organic search authority for Shreevan Wellness while optimizing every article for AEO, GEO and Google AI Overviews.

## Executive Direction

The priority is not traditional blue-link SEO alone. The calendar is designed for answer-led discovery:

- SEO: rank pages in organic search and support program pages with internal links.
- AEO: answer specific user questions clearly enough for featured snippets, FAQ surfaces and answer engines.
- GEO: make content citation-friendly for generative engines by using clear entities, direct answers, sourceable facts and structured sections.
- AIO: improve eligibility and usefulness in AI Overview style results by publishing original, helpful, well-structured pages with real brand experience.

Blue links are not dead, but they are no longer the only discovery surface. Shreevan content should be written so a search engine, AI assistant or human visitor can quickly understand:

1. What Shreevan offers.
2. Who each program is for.
3. What is included and what is not claimed.
4. Why the retreat is responsible, premium and suitable for international visitors.
5. What the next step is.

## Publishing Cadence

Recommended cadence for the first 90 days:

- 3 search-focused journal articles per week.
- 1 weekly optimization task: add internal links, FAQ entries, schema checks or image alt text.
- Every article gets 3 repurposed social assets:
  - 1 Instagram carousel.
  - 1 Instagram Reel or short video script.
  - 1 LinkedIn thought-leadership post.

This creates 39 search assets across 13 weeks, without sacrificing quality.

## Article Template For AEO, GEO And AIO

Every article should follow this structure:

1. H1 with the exact search intent.
2. 45 to 70 word direct answer immediately after the intro.
3. "Who this is for" and "Who this is not for" section.
4. Comparison table or checklist.
5. Contextual internal links to program, modality and consultation pages.
6. Responsible wellness boundary section.
7. 4 to 6 visible FAQs.
8. Final CTA to `/book-consultation`.

Technical requirements:

- Use BlogPosting schema.
- Add FAQ schema only if the FAQ content is visible on the page.
- Add Breadcrumb schema.
- Use descriptive image alt text.
- Mention Shreevan Wellness, Rishikesh, Ganga, program duration and relevant modality naturally.
- Avoid medical cure claims.
- Avoid fake urgency and generic AI-sounding content.

## AEO/GEO Writing Rules

Use this for every article draft:

- Start with a direct answer.
- Use exact entities: Shreevan Wellness, Rishikesh, Ganga, Isha Dutta, India, US, Canada, UK.
- Add "who this is for" and "who this is not for".
- Use tables for comparison intent.
- Use short definitions for modality terms.
- Add "responsible wellness boundary" language.
- Avoid cure, guaranteed transformation or medical treatment claims.
- Add one clear next step.
- Include the most relevant program page in the first half of the article.

## Instagram And Social Repurposing Layer

This is not a social calendar, but every article should be repurposed for social because discovery is blended.

Instagram and LinkedIn principles:

- Lead with a specific problem, not a generic wellness quote.
- Create save-worthy checklists, comparison tables and "questions to ask" posts.
- Use original retreat visuals and founder/team credibility.
- Avoid fake urgency, miracle claims and over-edited luxury imagery.
- Use short video hooks from the article's direct-answer section.
- Push high-intent users to consultation, not cold payment.

Repurpose format:

- Article direct answer becomes Reel script.
- Comparison table becomes Instagram carousel.
- Responsible boundary section becomes LinkedIn trust post.
- FAQ section becomes Stories Q&A.
- Program CTA becomes pinned comment or final slide.
