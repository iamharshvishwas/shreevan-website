## Why This Version Exists

The first draft was a usable topic map, but it was not strong enough as a 90-day specialist calendar. The weak point was distribution: "one reel, one carousel, one story" is not a strategy. A premium wellness retreat calendar needs search intent, AI-search answer design, proof assets, social angles, internal links and conversion paths working together.

This version treats every topic as an intent asset:

- Search asset: the journal article or landing-support article.
- Answer asset: the short direct answer built for AI Overviews, snippets and answer engines.
- Trust asset: founder quote, retreat image, proof block, safety note or guest-care detail.
- Distribution asset: specific Instagram, LinkedIn and email angle.
- Conversion asset: consultation CTA, program comparison, FAQ or contact prompt.

### Strategic Takeaways

1. Blue-link SEO is not dead, but it is no longer enough.
   Google still tells site owners to follow SEO best practices for AI search, but AI search behavior rewards pages that answer clearly, cite entities, structure information well and show real experience.

2. AI search uses expanded queries and retrieval behavior.
   A single article should answer the main query and the follow-up questions a human or AI assistant would ask next.

3. Wellness demand is not the issue. Trust and fit are the issue.
   Wellness travel and wellness economy demand remain large, but premium retreats have high friction: health claims, travel planning, payment confidence, room comfort, founder credibility and safety.

4. Shreevan should not chase viral wellness content.
   The brand needs fewer generic posts and more high-trust decision content for serious international guests.

5. Instagram/social distribution matters, but as demand warming, not as the SEO base.
   Instagram should turn search topics into save-worthy checklists, founder-led trust posts, objection-handling Reels and DM-to-consultation flows.

### Practical ROI Interpretation

- First 30 days: mostly asset creation and indexing. Do not expect serious revenue from SEO yet.
- Days 31-90: early impressions, long-tail clicks, first consultation signals.
- Months 4-6: first meaningful organic enquiries if pages are indexed, internally linked and updated.
- Months 7-12: compounding phase if the site publishes consistently and converts well.

High-confidence business logic:

- If one 28-day booking comes from organic content, revenue is around `$2,200`.
- If one 60-day residency comes from organic content, value should be materially higher, but do not model it until final price is confirmed.
- The calendar is worth doing if it creates even 2-4 qualified consultations per month by month 6, because the offer is high-consideration and premium.

## Content Architecture

### Funnel Roles

| Funnel stage | User state | Content job | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Awareness | "I need a reset" | Explain problem and retreat category | Read related article |
| Consideration | "Which retreat type fits me?" | Compare durations, modalities and locations | Explore program |
| Trust | "Can I trust this brand in India?" | Prove safety, founder, stay, food and boundaries | Contact team |
| Qualification | "Am I suitable?" | Help user self-select responsibly | Book consultation |
| Conversion | "I am close to booking" | Explain process, payment order and terms | Submit suitability request |

### Search Intent Isolation

- `/programs/*` owns duration and commercial program intent.
- `/modalities/*` owns educational practice intent.
- `/journal/*` owns questions, comparisons, objections and planning intent.
- `/faqs` owns short answer intent.
- `/book-consultation` owns conversion intent.
- `/payment` remains noindex and is for confirmed guests only.

## Specialist Publishing System

Weekly output:

- 2 major journal articles.
- 1 answer-led FAQ/short article.
- 1 conversion-support update or internal-linking task.
- 5 distribution assets extracted from the strongest weekly article.

This is stronger than 3 random articles per week because every week produces:

1. One ranking asset.
2. One AI-answer asset.
3. One trust proof.
4. One conversion bridge.
5. One social distribution pack.

## Article Format Standard

Every article must include:

- 50-70 word direct answer under the intro.
- "Best for" section.
- "Not right for" section.
- Comparison table or decision checklist.
- Responsible wellness boundary.
- 4-6 visible FAQs.
- Links to one program page, one modality page and one conversion page where relevant.
- BlogPosting schema.
- FAQ schema only when FAQs are visible.
- Original image or image placeholder with specific alt text.
- Founder/team quote where trust risk is high.
