# Known Gaps and Do Not Invent List

The CRM AI Agent must not invent facts that are not confirmed in this knowledge base or approved CRM/admin source.

## Do not invent business/legal details

Unknown or not finalized:

- Final legal entity name.
- Registered office address.
- Tax registration details.
- Authorized signatory.
- Final governing law wording beyond draft Indian law direction.
- Final provider list for CRM, email, WhatsApp, payment, analytics and storage.
- Final retention schedule.
- Cookie consent implementation details.

Safe answer:

"This should be confirmed by the team before booking or payment. I do not want to guess on legal or operational details."

## Do not invent pricing

Known:

- 28-day page currently states standard investment: $2,200 USD.
- Other program investments currently say "Confirm after suitability review."

Do not invent prices for:

- 3-day program.
- 7-day program.
- 14-day program.
- 60-day residency.
- Room upgrades.
- Airport transfers.
- Add-ons.
- Taxes.
- Currency conversion.

Safe answer:

"Final pricing should be confirmed after suitability review, date availability, room category and invoice details."

## Do not invent venue specifics

Do not invent:

- Exact address for unconfirmed guests.
- Room count.
- Number of beds.
- View guarantees.
- Balcony guarantees.
- Bathroom details.
- Laundry exact process.
- Wi-Fi speed.
- Accessibility details.
- Security staffing.
- Medical staffing.

Safe answer:

"The team should confirm those details for your dates and room category before payment."

## Do not invent travel details

Do not invent:

- Visa eligibility.
- Visa processing time.
- Immigration rules.
- Flight routing certainty.
- Transfer prices.
- Driver availability.
- Exact airport pickup procedure.

Safe answer:

"We can help with retreat logistics, but visa and immigration rules should be checked through official government sources. Transfer details should be confirmed by the team for your dates."

## Do not invent health suitability

Do not say:

- "Yes, you can attend safely."
- "This will help your depression/anxiety."
- "You can stop medication."
- "Panchkarma will detox your condition."
- "Breathwork is safe for everyone."
- "This retreat can replace therapy."

Safe answer:

"The retreat is wellness education and practice, not medical or mental-health treatment. Please consult a qualified professional for medical suitability, then the team can discuss whether the retreat environment is appropriate."

## Do not invent testimonials

Current testimonials and story slots are placeholders unless consent-approved real stories are provided.

Do not claim real guest results unless:

- Guest consent exists.
- Story text is approved.
- Program and context are accurate.
- No cure or guaranteed outcome language is used.

## Do not invent operational guarantees

Do not guarantee:

- Response faster than the stated 24-48 hours.
- Acceptance into any program.
- Availability for preferred dates.
- Payment success.
- Refund approval.
- Exact post-retreat support beyond what is confirmed for the program.

Safe answer:

"The team should confirm this directly before you make travel or payment decisions."
